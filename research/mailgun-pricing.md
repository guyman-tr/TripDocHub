# Mailgun Pricing Research

## Key Finding: Inbound Email is FREE!
From Mailgun blog: "we will no longer be charging for incoming emails"

## Plans (as of Dec 2024):

### Free Plan - $0/month
- 100 emails/day (sending)
- 1 inbound route
- 1 day log retention
- Ticket support

### Basic Plan - $15/month
- 10,000 emails/month
- 5 inbound routes
- 1 day log retention
- Extra emails: $1.80/1,000

### Foundation Plan - $35/month
- 50,000 emails/month
- Full access to inbound routing (unlimited routes)
- 5 days log retention
- Extra emails: $1.30/1,000

### Scale Plan - $90/month
- 100,000 emails/month
- Full inbound routing
- 30 days log retention
- Extra emails: $1.10/1,000

## For TripHub:
- Inbound email receiving is FREE
- We only need to pay if we want to SEND emails (notifications, etc.)
- Free plan with 1 inbound route is sufficient for testing
- Basic plan ($15/mo) gives 5 inbound routes - good for production
